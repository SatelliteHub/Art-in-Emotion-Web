Cookies are small text files containing a string of characters that can be placed on your computer or mobile device to uniquely identify your browser or device.

Cookies allow a site or service to recognize if your computer or device has visited that site or service before. They can be used to help understand how the site or service is being used, assist you in navigating between pages efficiently, remember your preferences, and generally improve your browsing experience. Cookies can also help ensure that the marketing you see online is more relevant to you and your interests.

This information helps us understand the average time spent on the site, the location of visitors, language preferences, and specific pages visited, allowing us to improve our website.
