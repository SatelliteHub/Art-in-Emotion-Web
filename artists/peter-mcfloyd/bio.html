Bergerac

Bergerac was born in France in 1962 in France.

He began to exhibit in the early 80s. His solo exhibitions were very successful, in Europe. In 1990, he exhibited in Japan with the same success and his exhibitions graudally moved away from Europe.

Bergerac's compositions are powerful. High-class monochrome canvases that fit perfectly into any contemporary interiors.
