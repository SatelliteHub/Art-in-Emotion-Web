Peter McFloy

Peter McFloy lives and creates in Ireland, yet his true passion lies in capturing the vibrant beauty of the East Coast, from Massachusetts to South Florida.

He is particularly inspired by the lighthouses of Martha's Vineyard and the sun-kissed beaches of Miami, where tropical colors and pristine white sands fuel his creativity. In Miami, he found a wealth of inspiration in the dramatic shapes and vivid hues of the lifeguard towers, which have become a signature element of South Beach.

McFloy's paintings embody the spirit of "optimistic futurism," radiating joy through bright colors, light, and an overall sense of positivity.
